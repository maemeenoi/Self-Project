About

I am a dedicated IT professional with a strong background in healthcare, currently completing my third year of a Bachelor of Information and Communication Technology at ARA. My unique journey began in the healthcare sector, where I spent years providing compassionate care, particularly for dementia patients. A work-related injury inspired me to transition into IT, driven by the desire to explore how technology can enhance healthcare systems and improve outcomes.

With expertise in SQL, Python, JavaScript, Java, and cloud computing (AWS), I combine my technical skills with real-world experience to tackle challenges at the intersection of technology and healthcare. I am currently working on the Mana Prostate Support Project, focused on raising awareness about prostate health and providing accessible resources to support affected individuals and families.

I am passionate about leveraging technology to innovate in healthcare, solve complex problems, and create meaningful impacts. Whether it’s through developing efficient systems or streamlining care processes, I am committed to making a difference.

Outside of work, I enjoy anime, photography, cooking, and exploring diverse cultures and languages. Let’s connect and explore how technology can transform lives!
